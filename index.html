Meet the Menace

We are the Transexual Menace, An Advocacy group for the rights nad liberties of the trans community in NYC and across the country. We seek to make our community a place where Trans people can live their lives in peace, through a combination of community outreach, peaceful protest, and mutual aid. We ask for dignity and equal treatment, nothing more, and nothing less.

If you are interested in helping us advocate for trans rights, particularly via the following methods, scan the qr code for more information

  • Mutual Aid projects to ensure a thriving, healthy community
  • Community defense projects, to ensure that all trans New Yorkers can feel safe in our city
  • Political protest, to ensure that the rights and dignity of trans people remain a priority of those in power
  • Community outreach, so that trans people may connect with our cis neighbors and allies
